7 days Leh Ladakh road trip with Turtuk Overview

Embark on a 7-day Ladakh adventure, starting with Leh's acclimatization and local sightseeing. Explore Nubra Valley's mesmerizing landscapes, visit the remote village of Turtuk, and witness the ever-changing hues of Pangong Tso. The itinerary combines cultural immersion, high-altitude landscapes, and serene moments, ensuring an unforgettable journey through Ladakh's natural wonders. The tour culminates in Leh, leaving travelers with cherished memories of this Himalayan paradise.
